Cookie Policy

Last updated: 30 May 2026

Cookies are small text files that a website can store on your device. This page explains how cookies are — and aren't — used on this site.

Cookies we set

None. This website does not set any cookies of its own. We use no advertising cookies, no analytics cookies, and no tracking of any kind. You don't need to accept anything to browse it.

Third-party requests

The page loads typefaces from Google Fonts. Google Fonts does not set cookies, but your browser does request the font files from Google's servers, which may log technical data such as your IP address. You can read more in Google's privacy policy.

The site also links to external platforms such as Instagram and Strava. These services may set their own cookies, but only once you actually click through and visit them — not while you are on this site.

Managing cookies

Because we don't set any, there's nothing to clear here. If you'd like to control cookies in general, every browser lets you block or delete them in its settings. Doing so will not affect how this site works.

Changes

If we ever start using cookies — for example by adding analytics — we will update this page and add a proper consent banner beforehand.
